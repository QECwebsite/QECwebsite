Meet the QEC Team

The Queen's Engineering Competition is organized by a passionate team of student leaders dedicated to fostering innovation, professionalism, and excellence. Meet our Co-Chairs, Directors, and Coordinators.

  • Strategy: Directors translate goals into actionable plans.
  • Execution: Coordinators support competitors and sponsors throughout QEC.
